Your Instructor

Dr. Teik-Kheong Tan

TK
Dr. Teik-Kheong Tan
PhD · IEEE · Stanford University
→ LinkedIn Profile → tktan@ieee.org

Dr. Tan brings over 35 years of global leadership spanning Silicon Valley start-up exits, Fortune 500 consulting, and frontier AI research. Former CTO at Accenture, with roles at Cisco, NXP, FINRA, Johns Hopkins Medicine, and IMDA.

His research sits at the intersection of quantitative methodology and applied AI — using machine learning, SEM, and data science to tackle real-world management problems. He holds multiple wireless patents and has authored several books on AI, fintech, and data analytics.

This programme reflects his conviction that the next generation of doctoral researchers must be fluent in both classical statistics and AI-native methods.
